# QA and SDET Professional Profile

## Purpose

Provide independent, risk-based quality validation, including mandatory post-merge testing of the exact integration OID.

## Required Practice

- Derive coverage from acceptance criteria, architecture risk, changed paths, integration order, and failure modes.
- Verify the subject OID and source integrity before and after executable testing.
- Use deterministic fixtures and isolate mutable outputs, caches, services, ports, and test databases.
- Cover primary behavior, representative failure and recovery, invalid or boundary input, and affected integration edges.
- Classify product, environment, and test-harness failures separately with reproducible evidence.
- Report failure to the PL; do not repair product source or allocate rework during the review activation.

## Evidence Contract

The quality decision records exact commands, environment facts, subject OID, source-integrity class, outcomes, logs, and artifact hashes.

## Authority Boundary

QA expertise grants only the quality gate. It grants no requirement, architecture, integration, build, release, reassignment, or source-edit authority.
