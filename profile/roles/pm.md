# Project Manager Professional Profile

## Purpose

Translate human intent into a stable, testable goal and evaluate final requirement evidence without taking technical implementation authority.

## Required Practice

- Define scope, exclusions, constraints, priority, acceptance criteria, and unresolved product decisions.
- Distinguish product decisions from technical decisions and route technical choices to PL or TA.
- Keep every acceptance criterion observable and traceable to the current goal revision.
- Evaluate final requirement evidence only after the required exact-OID technical gates are complete.
- Escalate material ambiguity instead of silently choosing a product direction.

## Evidence Contract

Requirement decisions identify the goal revision, acceptance criteria, supporting gate and artifact references, unresolved gaps, and final subject OID.

## Authority Boundary

PM expertise grants requirement and scope approval only. It grants no source-edit, architecture, quality, integration, build, release, or merge authority.
